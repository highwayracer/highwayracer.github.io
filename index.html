Features:

Highway racer currently has 4 game modes:

1. OneWay

You will control your favorite car on the highway with 2 super wide lanes.

2. TwoWay

You will control your favorite car on the highway with 2 lanes in opposite directions. Every time you go into the opposite lane, you will get bonus points, but there is also the risk of a game-ending accident.

3.Time Attack

This is an extended version of OneWay. You will control your car on the highway within 100 seconds. You will have to pass a lot of other cars to get the best score

4. Speed Bomb

This is an extended version of one way. You will control your car and carry a bomb that can explode if your speed is below 100 km/h. You must continuously maintain your speed above 100km/h for the new car to not explode. If the car explodes you will end the game
